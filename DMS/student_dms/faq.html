The co-op

Cooperative education is a structured method of combining classroom-based education with practical work experience. A cooperative education experience, commonly known as a “co-op”, provides academic credit for structured job experience.

Learn About Co-op Process

The Co-op Process Successful co-ops start with preparation at the beginning of your academic studies leading up to your co-op placement term. In order to maximize your success, we broke down the co-op process into four modules: Career Prep, Co-op Orientation, Co-op Term, and Graduation.

Try to take each module step-by-step, and don’t get overwhelmed by trying to complete all the steps at once. Each module indicates when you should focus on each area. Once you click “begin now” you will see the recommended steps to complete each module that will ultimately help you prepare for your co-op, and successfully land your co-op placement.
